Development-only

It's important to note that I'm a web designer first and a web developer second. While I'm very capable and well-practiced in custom web development, I do not offer it as a stand-alone service. I will develop only when it supports my design work.

Hosting and domain names

You should always be in control of your own domain name and hosting. I will provide you with recommendations when needed.

Anything to do with email*

There are too many issues that could go wrong with email and I'm not an expert. I don't recommend your email server be the same as your web server. Contact your IT professional for more information.

* Except when a contact form is involved.

Website Maintenance

I create most sites with WordPress which has an easy-to-use update system. Regularly check your dashboard each month for new updates.

Search Engine Optimization

I've been working on the web for a long time and had, at one time, offered SEO services. I don't do so anymore. You should hire someone who specializes in this in addition to a content strategist.
